About Me

I am Fatema Khan, currently working as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB). Previously, I worked as a Lecturer in the department of CSE at Green University of Bangladesh for 1 year. I pursued my M.Sc and B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Jahangirnagar University, Bangladesh.
